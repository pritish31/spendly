Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. You can request a full export or permanent deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts for typography. No expense or account data is shared with third-party services. If this changes, we will update this policy and notify users in advance.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
